Cookie Care & FAQ

Everything you need to know about caring for your custom cookies

How long do decorated cookies stay fresh?

Our decorated sugar cookies stay fresh for up to 2 weeks when stored in an airtight container at room temperature. For best taste, enjoy within the first week!

Can I freeze the cookies?

Yes! Decorated cookies freeze beautifully for up to 3 months. Place in airtight containers with parchment between layers. Thaw at room temperature when ready to enjoy.

Do you have any dietary restrictions I should know about?

Our cookies contain gluten, eggs, and dairy. Please let us know about any allergies or dietary concerns when placing your order so we can discuss ingredients and ensure they're safe for your guests.

How should I store my cookies?

Store in a cool, dry place in an airtight container. Avoid refrigeration as it can cause condensation. Keep away from direct sunlight to preserve the beautiful icing colors.

What's your minimum order?

We typically work with orders starting at one dozen cookies, but we're happy to discuss smaller quantities for special circumstances. Each order is custom-quoted based on your needs.

Do you deliver?

Yes! We offer local delivery within 20 miles for a small fee. We also provide convenient pickup options at our location. Delivery details will be arranged during your consultation.
